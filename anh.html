About Animal Health

Animal Health is a technical course offered at Bigogwe TSS under TVET education. It focuses on livestock management, animal disease prevention, veterinary basics, and modern farming techniques.

Importance of Animal Health in Education

Food Security

Healthy animals provide safe meat, milk, and eggs for communities.

Economic Growth

Livestock farming creates employment and supports local businesses.

Disease Prevention

Students learn how to control and prevent animal diseases.

Courses Offered

  • Animal Nutrition
  • Veterinary Laboratory Skills
  • Animal Breeding
  • Livestock Production
  • Animal Disease Control

Career Opportunities

Graduates from Bigogwe TSS Animal Health department can become:

  • Veterinary Assistants
  • Farm Managers
  • Animal Health Technicians
  • Livestock Officers
