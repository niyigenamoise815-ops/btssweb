About Agriculture in TVET

At Bigogwe TSS, agriculture education is integrated with practical skills to prepare students for careers in farming, agribusiness, and sustainable development. Our TVET programs focus on hands-on learning and innovation.

Importance of Agriculture Education

Food Security

Education in agriculture helps communities produce sufficient food and promotes sustainable farming.

Economic Growth

Equipping students with skills to develop agribusiness contributes to local and national economies.

Environmental Sustainability

Students learn sustainable practices that protect natural resources and biodiversity.
